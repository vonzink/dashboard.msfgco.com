# Loans Funded

Dashboard section: fundedLoans

## What it is
Loans Funded is the archive of loans that have closed and funded. Each row shows the borrower, loan amount, loan officer, group, product, and funded date. A summary bar above the table shows total units and total volume for the current filters.

## How to find it
Scroll to the "Loans Funded" section on the dashboard home page (the last of the 6 scroll sections). The section header has board and group filter dropdowns, a time-period dropdown, and a "Sync" button.

## Common tasks

### Filter by time period
Use the period dropdown to view Weekly, Monthly (default), Quarterly, Year to Date, or All Time funded loans. Your last-used period is remembered on your next visit.

### Filter by board or group
Use the "All Boards" / "All Groups" dropdowns to narrow the list to a specific Monday.com board or loan-officer group. Changing the board resets the group filter, since available groups can differ per board.

### Sync from Monday.com
Click "Sync" in the section header to pull newly funded loans in from Monday.com.

### Check total units and volume for a period
The summary bar above the table updates automatically with the filtered period/board/group — no separate action needed, just adjust the filters above it.

## FAQ
**Q: What counts as a "funded" loan here?**
A: Loans that have been synced into the funded-loans archive from Monday.com boards mapped to the Funded Loans section, filtered by their funded date.

**Q: Why did the list change when I picked a different board?**
A: The group filter resets whenever you switch boards, since each board can have a different set of groups — pick your group again after choosing a board.

**Q: Does this feed the Goals numbers on the home page?**
A: Yes — Volume Closed and Loans Closed goal tiles pull from the same funded-loans data, filtered to your own loans and the goal period you've selected.

# Pre-Approvals

Dashboard section: preApprovals

## What it is
The Pre-Approvals section lists borrowers who've received a pre-approval letter, populated primarily by syncing from Monday.com. Each row shows the client name, loan amount, pre-approval and expiration dates, status, loan officer, property address, and loan type.

## How to find it
Scroll to the "Pre-Approvals" section on the dashboard home page (below News & Announcements). The section header has board/group filter dropdowns and a "Sync" button.

## Common tasks

### Sync from Monday.com
Click "Sync" in the section header to pull the latest pre-approvals from the connected Monday.com board(s).

### View and edit a pre-approval's details
Click a row to open its detail view, which shows the full record (property, loan purpose, occupancy, rate, credit score, income, LTV/DTI, referring agent, and notes). From there you can click **Edit** to update fields, or **Delete** to remove the record. Edits and deletes write through to Monday.com when the record is linked to a Monday item (the delete archives the item on Monday.com rather than just removing it locally).

### Filter by board or group
Use the "All Boards" / "All Groups" dropdowns in the section header to narrow the list — useful when multiple Monday boards or loan-officer groups feed into pre-approvals.

### Add notes to a pre-approval
Inside the detail view, add time-stamped notes visible to the team. Only the note's author or an admin can edit or delete it afterward.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I create a pre-approval manually, or does it only come from Monday.com sync?**
A: New records are typically added by syncing from Monday.com. Existing records can be edited or deleted from the loan's detail view in the dashboard, and those changes write back to Monday.com when linked.

**Q: I deleted a pre-approval — did it also disappear from Monday.com?**
A: If the record was linked to a Monday.com item, deleting it in the dashboard also archives the item on Monday.com (rather than leaving a stale record there).

**Q: Why don't I see the board/group filter dropdowns?**
A: They only appear once pre-approval data has loaded and there's more than one board or group to filter by.

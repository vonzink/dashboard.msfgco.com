# Notifications & Reminders

Dashboard section: (none — bell icon in the header → Notifications modal)

## What it is
Notifications are personal reminders you set for yourself — a date, time, and note, with a choice of how you'd like to be reminded and whether it repeats. They're separate from Team Chat and Announcements; nothing here is shared with other users.

## How to find it
Click the bell icon in the dashboard's top header (`Notifications` tooltip) to open the Notifications modal. It lists your existing reminders and has a button to add a new one.

## Common tasks

### View your reminders
Open the Notifications modal (bell icon). Each reminder shows its note, date/time, a delivery badge (Email, Text, or both), and a recurrence badge if it repeats. If you have none yet, it shows "No reminders set up yet."

### Add a reminder
In the modal, click the toggle to open the add-reminder form. Fill in a date, a time, and a note (what you want to be reminded about), then choose:
- **Delivery**: Email, Text Message, or Both
- **Recurrence**: One-time, Daily, Weekly, or Monthly

Save it, and it appears in your reminder list immediately.

### Delete a reminder
Click the trash icon on a reminder. You'll be asked to confirm ("Delete this reminder?") before it's removed.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I set a reminder for someone else?**
A: Regular users can only create reminders for themselves. Admins can create a reminder on behalf of another user.

**Q: Can a reminder repeat automatically?**
A: Yes — set Recurrence to Daily, Weekly, or Monthly instead of One-time when creating it.

**Q: Are my reminders visible to my team?**
A: No. Notifications are private to your own account — they're not shared with Team Chat, Announcements, or other users.

**Q: What delivery options are available?**
A: Email, Text Message, or Both, chosen when you create the reminder.

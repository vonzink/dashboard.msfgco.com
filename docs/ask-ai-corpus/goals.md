# Goals

Dashboard section: goals

## What it is
Goals is the performance ribbon at the top of the dashboard home page: four tiles — Loans Closed, Volume Closed, Pipeline, and Pre-Approvals — each showing your current number against a target you set, with a progress bar. A fifth tile shows a mini chart of recent closings plus year-to-date units, volume, and pull-through stats.

## How to find it
It's the first section on the dashboard home page, right below the header. A period selector (Monthly, Quarterly, Year to Date, Weekly) lets you change what timeframe the tiles reflect. Admins and managers also get a loan-officer picker to view another LO's goal performance.

## Common tasks

### Set a target
Click the pencil icon on any goal tile (Loans Closed, Volume Closed, Pipeline, or Pre-Approvals) to open the target-edit modal, enter a number, and save. Volume and Pipeline targets are entered in millions (e.g. enter 5 for a $5M target).

### Set targets from Settings instead
Open Settings (gear icon in the header) → Goals tab. It shows the same four goal cards with number inputs — set a target and it saves on blur or Enter, and updates the home-page tiles too. This tab has its own period selector, independent of which period you last viewed on the home page.

### Change the reporting period
Use the period dropdown in the Goals section header (or in Settings → Goals) to switch between Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Year to Date. Your choice is remembered for next time.

### View another loan officer's performance (admin/manager)
Use the loan-officer dropdown next to the period selector to switch the tiles to a specific LO's numbers instead of your own. This picker only appears for admin/manager roles.

## FAQ
**Q: Where do I set my monthly closing target?**
A: Click the pencil icon on the "Loans Closed" tile in the Goals section, or go to Settings → Goals and enter a target there — both save the same target.

**Q: Why is my Volume Closed target asking for a small number like "5" instead of a dollar amount?**
A: Volume and Pipeline targets are entered in millions — enter 5 to mean $5,000,000.

**Q: The Goals tiles show someone else's numbers — how do I get back to mine?**
A: If you're an admin/manager and used the loan-officer picker to view a teammate's goals, switch it back to your own name (or the "All Loan Officers" option) in that same dropdown.
